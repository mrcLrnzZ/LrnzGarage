Our Services in LRNZ Garage




At LRNZ Garage, we are dedicated to providing an unparalleled experience in luxury car rental services. Our commitment to excellence extends beyond just offering exceptional vehicles; it encompasses every aspect of your journey with us. Explore our comprehensive range of services tailored to meet your every need:


Luxury Car Rental: Experience the thrill of driving your dream car with our extensive fleet of luxury vehicles. From iconic brands like Mercedes-Benz and Ferrari to the exclusive elegance of Rolls-Royce, we offer a diverse selection to suit every preference and occasion.

Personalized Concierge: Our knowledgeable staff is committed to providing personalized assistance at every step of your rental journey. Whether you need help selecting the perfect vehicle or arranging special accommodations, our concierge services ensure that every detail is taken care of with the utmost care and attention.

Flexible Rental Options: Whether you're planning a weekend getaway, attending a special event, or simply indulging in the luxury of driving your dream car, LRNZ Garage offers flexible rental options to suit your schedule and preferences.

Delivery and Pickup: For added convenience, we offer delivery and pickup services to your desired location. Whether you're at home, a hotel, or an event venue, our team will ensure that your vehicle is delivered and collected promptly and efficiently.

Corporate Services: Elevate your corporate events and business trips with our corporate rental services. Impress clients, partners, and colleagues with our prestigious fleet of luxury vehicles, tailored to meet the demands of your professional endeavors.

Event Transportation: Make a grand entrance at weddings, galas, and other special occasions with our event transportation services. Our chauffeurs will ensure that you arrive in style, leaving a lasting impression on all who witness your arrival.

Long-Term Rentals: Planning an extended stay or looking for a long-term rental solution? LRNZ Garage offers competitive rates and flexible terms for long-term rentals, allowing you to enjoy the luxury of our vehicles for as long as you desire.


Step into our meticulously designed space, and you'll find yourself surrounded by the finest automobiles the industry has to offer. From the timeless elegance of Mercedes-Benz to the unrivaled performance of Ferrari, our collection encompasses the essence of luxury, craftsmanship, and innovation.

At LRNZ Garage, we understand that every driver is unique, which is why we offer a diverse selection of vehicles from each brand, ensuring that there's something to suit every preference and occasion. Whether you're seeking the exhilarating thrill of a Lamborghini or the refined sophistication of a Rolls-Royce, our fleet of ten cars per brand promises to exceed your expectations.
